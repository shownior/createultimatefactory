About Create: Ultimate Factory Bedrock Edition

It's a simple addon for Create that introduces new automation possibilities. Through new (reasonably) balanced recipes, this mod makes dozens of Minecraft resources renewable.

For example, Tufo is now renewable, offering players a reliable source of Ores and Experience. This also includes rare items and loot, at a much lower rate.

Ever dreamed of automating Diamond or Netherite? Now it's possible!

IMPORTANT! Installation Requirements

To use this addon, ensure you follow these steps exactly:

  1. 1 Create a new world or edit an existing one.
  2. 2 Open the Experiments section.
  3. 3 Turn on Beta APIs. (The addon will not work without this enabled!)
  4. 4 For players around versions 26.20+, you must also enable the Upcoming Creator Features toggle.
💡

For Create Addon Players by Vatonage: The app can sometimes be buggy with caching. Make sure to delete your old downloaded addon (CREATE ADDON BY VATONAGE) from your files first before downloading and importing the new one.
